# About TRISPECTIVE  

Hello! I’m a Rana, and TRISPECTIVE is my digital diary - a space for  an extension of my thoughts, and evolving understanding.

---

## 🧩 Why the name "TRISPECTIVE"?

“TRISPECTIVE” stands for **The Third Perspective**.

Most things in the world are seen in duality — two sides, two extremes. For example, Religion and Science are often treated as opposing forces. People tend to choose one side.

But I believe truth does not fully belong to either side.  
It often exists in the *pattern between them* — a perspective we usually ignore.

This platform is an attempt to explore that third angle.

---

## Why Blogging in the Era of AI?

I believe in being transparent: I do use AI while writing—but as a **strategic partner, not a writer**.

AI helps with the **“body”** of my articles—structuring content, improving presentation, and fixing grammar or translation errors. It handles the heavy lifting.

But the **“soul”**—the core ideas, flow, and the “third perspective” that connects information—comes entirely from me.

I don’t write just to share information. Blogging helps me clarify my thoughts, reconnect with my ideas, and deepen my understanding.

This blog is as much for my growth as it is for yours.

--- 

## 🔍 What you’ll find here

This website is not limited to one subject. Instead, it brings together a unique mix of ideas from different fields, such as:

- **Philosophy** — Deep questions about life and existence  
- **Religion** — Understanding the logic behind traditions  
- **Science** — Laws that govern the universe and nature  
- **Society** — Human behavior and the world around us  

My goal is not just to present information, but to **connect scattered ideas**, identify patterns, and present them from a fresh perspective.

---

## 💡 My Perspective (Not Absolute Truth)

Everything written here reflects my **current understanding and exploration**.

It is not the ultimate truth.

You are completely free to disagree — and your disagreement is respected.  
There may be mistakes or gaps in my understanding. If you notice anything, feel free to point it out.

Your feedback plays an important role in my self-development.

---

## 🎯 The Real Purpose of This Site

To be honest, this website is not created to lead any movement or to “teach wisdom.”

It is a **personal project**.

I built it because I wanted to — despite not knowing how at first.  
Whenever I come across something interesting or worth sharing, I’ll write about it here.

**Consistency?** There is none.

Sometimes there may be multiple posts in a week, and sometimes silence for months — or even years. It entirely depends on my curiosity and mood.
